Our story

Built for long-term service, not one-time sales.

Summit Climate Systems began in 1998 with a small service crew and a straightforward idea: diagnose the equipment carefully, explain the options clearly, and let the homeowner decide. That approach helped the company grow through referrals from customers who wanted a familiar local team rather than a rotating call center.

Today we work on residential and light commercial systems across the region. The tools and equipment have changed; the way we handle a service call has not. Customers receive a written estimate before work begins, a clear record of what was completed, and a local number to call if they have a question afterward.
