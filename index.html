What We Do

What We Fix & Install

Whether it's a dead compressor in August or a furnace that won't ignite in January, our NATE-certified crew handles residential and light commercial work across the greater Summit City area.

Air Conditioning

Central systems, ductless mini-splits, and rooftop units. We size equipment correctly, handle refrigerant by the book, and don't push replacements you don't need.

Heating Systems

Gas furnaces, boilers, and heat pumps from Carrier, Trane, Lennox, and Rheem. Every heating call includes a combustion safety check — no shortcuts.

24/7 Emergency Repair

No heat at 6 AM? AC out on a holiday? Our dispatch line is staffed 24/7. Trucks carry common parts so most breakdowns get fixed in a single visit.
