Air Conditioning Repair & Installation

South Carolina summers punish undersized and neglected AC units. We work on central split systems, ductless mini-splits, and commercial rooftop units — running proper load calculations so you're not paying to cool air you don't need.

Services Include

  • New central AC sizing, installation, and duct modifications
  • Ductless mini-splits for garages, additions, and older homes
  • Compressor, capacitor, and refrigerant leak diagnostics
  • Smart thermostat setup — Ecobee, Nest, Honeywell Home
  • Spring tune-ups before the first heat wave hits
  • SEER upgrade consultations with utility rebate guidance

How We're Different

  • Manual-J load calculations — we don't guess at tonnage
  • EPA Section 608 certified for refrigerant recovery
  • Same-day slots held open June through August
  • 0% financing on qualifying Carrier and Trane installs
  • 10-year parts warranty on select new systems

Furnace & Heating Repair

A furnace that won't fire on a cold night isn't just uncomfortable — it can be unsafe. We service gas and electric furnaces, boilers, and heat pumps, and we check carbon monoxide levels on every heating visit.

Services Include

  • Furnace repair, replacement, and new construction installs
  • Boiler service for radiant floor and baseboard systems
  • Air-source heat pumps and dual-fuel hybrid setups
  • Heat exchanger and flue inspections
  • Duct sealing to fix cold rooms and high gas bills
  • Thermostat and zoning upgrades for two-story homes

How We're Different

  • Factory training on Carrier, Trane, Lennox, and Rheem
  • Fall tune-up appointments booked before the first freeze
  • Flat-rate pricing posted for common repairs — no hourly surprises
  • Help filing Duke Energy and local utility rebates
  • 24/7 no-heat dispatch when outdoor temps drop below 32°F

HVAC Maintenance Plans

Most emergency calls we run could have been prevented with a $150 tune-up six months earlier. Our membership plans cover two visits per year, filter changes, and priority scheduling when everyone's AC breaks at once.

What's Covered

  • Spring AC and fall furnace inspections (two visits/year)
  • Standard filter replacement at each visit
  • Blower motor, capacitor, and contactor checks
  • Refrigerant charge verification and coil cleaning
  • Written condition report with photos after every visit
  • 15% off repairs, 5% off new equipment for members

Member Perks

  • No after-hours surcharges on plan-covered calls

  • Annual email summary of your system's condition
  • Automatic reminders — we call you, not the other way around

Indoor Air Quality Services

Dust, pet dander, and humidity problems don't fix themselves. We install whole-home purifiers, humidifiers, and ERV systems — and we actually test your air before recommending equipment, instead of selling the most expensive filter on the truck.

What We Offer

  • HEPA and UV air purification system installation
  • Whole-home humidifiers and dehumidifiers
  • Professional duct cleaning and sanitization
  • Energy recovery ventilators (ERV) and HRV systems
  • Indoor air quality testing and pollutant identification
  • Carbon monoxide and radon detector integration

Ideal For

  • Allergy and asthma sufferers seeking relief at home
  • Homes with pets, smoke, or persistent odors
  • New construction requiring ventilation compliance
  • Offices and retail spaces with high occupancy
  • Properties with moisture, mold, or dust concerns
